DATA DESCRIPTION
genre: The musical category to which the track belongs.
artist_name: The name of the artist who performed the track.
track_name: The title of the track.
track_id: Unique identifier for each track.
popularity: A measure of how popular the track is on Spotify.
acousticness: A measure of the acoustic characteristics of the track.
danceability: Indicates how suitable the track is for dancing based on tempo, rhythm stability, beat strength, and overall regularity.
duration_ms: The duration of the track in milliseconds.
energy: Represents the intensity and activity level of the track.
instrumentalness: Predicts whether a track contains no vocals.
key: The key the track is in.
liveness: Detects the presence of an audience in the recording.
loudness: The overall loudness of the track in decibels (dB).
mode: Indicates the modality (major or minor) of the track.
speechiness: Detects the presence of spoken words in the track.
tempo: The overall estimated tempo of the track in beats per minute (BPM).
time_signature: An estimate of how many beats are in each bar.
valence: Describes the musical positiveness conveyed by the track.

This dataset allows for exploring how these features correlate with valence, which measures the musical positivity conveyed by a track, thereby shedding light on the emotional and qualitative aspects of music across different genres.
